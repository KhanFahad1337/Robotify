About Robotify

A passionate team of engineers, makers, and educators based in Karachi — building the future one circuit at a time.

Our Story

Built by Engineers
for Engineers

Robotify was founded with a simple mission: make cutting-edge engineering services accessible to students, startups, and businesses in Pakistan. We noticed that too many students were struggling with their final year projects, and too many businesses couldn't find affordable local expertise in robotics and IoT.

We bridged that gap. From Arduino prototypes to complete solar installations — Robotify delivers precision engineering with a personal touch, right here in Karachi.
